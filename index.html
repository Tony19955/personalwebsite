About me

Started as 12 years old with 2D graphics and was contributing to chmatakov.cz as a member of lead graphic team. Later got initiated to 3D and achieve rich experience with multidimensional modeling.

 

One day, came opportunity to make a first website and that's when my web development journey began. I dove into studying, tested the results of every new piece of knowledge, analyzed the performance and applied in real projects.

Nowdays, I work as passionate freelance full-stack web developer using the latest code practices and developing my own e-commerce solution.

 

"I learnt to love DRY rule, but even more KISS (Keep it stupid simple), that's why you can find my code clean, debugged and easy maintainable"
